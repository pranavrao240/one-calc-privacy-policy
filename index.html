01

Overview

One-Calc ("we", "our", or "us") operates the One-Calc mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

One-Calc is a premium calculator application. Our core principle is simple: we collect as little data as possible. The app is designed to work entirely on your device without needing to transmit your information to external servers.

🛡️

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, location, contacts, payment data, or any identifiers that could be used to identify you.

02

Information we collect

We collect only the minimum information required for the app to function. All data stays on your device.

🎙️

Microphone access

Used solely for speech-to-text input. Audio is processed on-device and never transmitted or stored externally.

⚙️

App preferences

Theme and display settings stored locally on your device only. Never sent to us or any third party.

📋

Calculation history

Stored locally on your device. You can clear it at any time from within the app settings.

🚫

Nothing else

No account required. No sign-in. No identifiers, analytics IDs, or device fingerprinting.

03

Speech recognition

When you use the voice input feature, the App requests permission to access your microphone. Speech is processed using on-device APIs provided by your operating system:

Android — uses Android's SpeechRecognizer API.
iOS — uses Apple's SFSpeechRecognizer and AVAudioSession APIs.

We do not access, store, record, or transmit any audio. You may revoke microphone permission at any time in your device's Settings app without affecting any other app functionality.

ℹ️

On iOS, Apple may process speech via their servers depending on your device settings. Refer to Apple's privacy policy for details on their speech processing practices.

04

Third-party services

One-Calc does not integrate any third-party analytics platforms, advertising SDKs, crash reporting services (that transmit data externally), or tracking libraries.

No user data is shared with, sold to, or rented to any third parties — ever. We have no advertising partners or data brokers.

05

Children's privacy

One-Calc does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The app is a general-purpose calculator suitable for all ages.

Because we collect no personal data from any user, there is no specific risk to younger users. Parents and guardians can allow use of One-Calc with confidence.

06

Data security

Since all data — calculation history and preferences — is stored locally on your device, security depends on your device's own protections (screen lock, encryption, etc.).

We do not operate any servers that store your personal data, which means there is no central database that could be breached. The safest data is data we never have.

07

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date at the top. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
